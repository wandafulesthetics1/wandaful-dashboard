Wells Fargo Import

Export your bank transactions, drop the CSV here, and check off only your business expenses.

How to export from Wells Fargo
1
Go to wellsfargo.com → sign in → click your checking account
2
Click Download Account Activity (above your transactions list)
3
Select your date range and choose Comma-Separated Values (CSV)
4
Save the file and drop it below — then check only your business expenses
Import CSV

Defaults to expenses only since your Square Import already handles income. Income rows are hidden and unchecked by default — use the filter to show them if needed.
